Wheat Rust Genomic Improvement

We are funded by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation on the grant "Wheat Rust Genomic Improvement to Enable Improved Crop Protection Strategies", grant number OPPGD1491. In this project, we are working with Dr. Cristina Cuomo at the Broad Insitute and Dr. Les Szabo at the United States Department of Argiculture's Agricultural Research Service to develop genomic resources for this important plant pathogen.
